About Our KidsAcademy

Hello Kids Maruthankuzhi is a renowned Montessori-based educational school, where children can experience the joy of learning. Our classrooms and equipment are designed to make children curious, independent, and love discovering new things. We have qualified teachers who create a supportive and interesting environment. They help children feel confident and think critically. We understand that every child develops at their own pace , so we allow them to learn at their own speed. Our education focuses on academics, social and emotional development, and practical life skills. We believe in keeping strong communication with parents and involving them in their child's education. Our campus is colourful, safe, and exciting for children to explore and learn. We provide age-appropriate materials, outdoor play areas, and activities that match each child's interests and needs. Come and see how Montessori education at Hello Kids Maruthankuzhi Montessori School can transform your child's future success. Discover the joy of learning, independence, and endless.

Active Learning

At Hello Kids Montessori, we believe in promoting active learning through entertaining and interactive activities, enabling children to actively learn , discover, and form meaningful connections with the world around them.

Expert Teachers

The heart of Hello Kids is our dedicated and certified instructors, who encourage a lifetime love of learning in every child who enters our family through their training, passion, and support.

Strategy Teachers

We establish close relationships with parents at Hello Kids, bridging the home-school divide through interactive collaboration with teachers and actively combining parents in their child's educational path for complete growth.

Full Day Programs

In order to encourage the growth of basic abilities and a lifelong love of learning, our full-day programming at Hello Kids offers an education that combines interesting classes, creative study, outdoor play, healthy meals, relaxation time, and lasting relationships.
