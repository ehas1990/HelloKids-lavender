LKG / Montessori-2

In the Nursery, children actively engage in school-readiness activities, including reading , writing, counting, number recognition, and problem-solving. These activities are thoughtfully designed to be enjoyable and advantageous, fostering a well-rounded learning environment. Additionally, children are encouraged to reinforce their learning through collaborative activities in various fields, such as theater, science, and the arts.
